Holiday Destination


The beautiful Chikhaldara hill station is in the Amravati district surrounded by several tourist spots. It is 1088 metres above sea level, the only coffee-producing hill station in the region and enriched with a variety of flora and fauna amidst the alluring beauty of nature.
